1. Introduction

ZIP Territory Pro ("we," "our," or "us") operates the ZIP Territory Pro platform that provides social media content generation and management services for real estate professionals. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our service.

2. Information We Collect

Account Information

  • Email address and password
  • First and last name
  • Real estate territory information (ZIP codes)

Social Media Integration

  • Instagram profile information (username, account type)
  • Access tokens for posting to your connected social media accounts
  • Content you generate and post through our platform

Usage Information

  • Content generation history and preferences
  • Platform usage analytics
  • Device and browser information

3. How We Use Your Information

  • Service Provision: To generate personalized social media content based on your territory
  • Social Media Integration: To post content directly to your connected social media accounts
  • Account Management: To maintain your account and provide customer support
  • Platform Improvement: To analyze usage patterns and improve our services
  • Communication: To send you service-related updates and notifications

4. Information Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personal information to third parties except in the following circumstances:

  • Social Media Platforms: When you authorize us to post content to your social media accounts
  • Service Providers: With trusted third parties who assist in operating our platform (hosting, analytics)
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law or to protect our rights and safety
  • Business Transfers: In connection with a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets

5. Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your personal information:

  • Encryption of sensitive data in transit and at rest
  • Secure access controls and authentication
  • Regular security audits and updates
  • Limited access to personal information on a need-to-know basis

6. Social Media Integration

When you connect your social media accounts (such as Instagram):

  • We only access the minimum permissions necessary for posting content
  • We store access tokens securely and encrypted
  • You can disconnect your accounts at any time
  • We do not access or store your private messages or followers
  • Posted content is subject to the respective social media platform's terms and privacy policies

7. Your Rights and Choices

You have the following rights regarding your personal information:

  • Access: Request a copy of your personal information
  • Correction: Update or correct inaccurate information
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your account and associated data
  • Portability: Request your data in a portable format
  • Disconnect: Remove social media account connections at any time

8. Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as necessary to provide our services and comply with legal obligations:

  • Account information: Until you delete your account
  • Content generation history: Up to 2 years for service improvement
  • Social media tokens: Until you disconnect the account or tokens expire

9. Children's Privacy

Our services are not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you become aware that a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately.
